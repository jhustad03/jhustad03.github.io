Pioneers of AI

Alan Turing

Known as the father of AI, Turing developed the Turing Test to measure machine intelligence.

John McCarthy

Coined the term "Artificial Intelligence" and developed the Lisp programming language.

Geoffrey Hinton

Deep learning pioneer, responsible for neural network advancements and AI breakthroughs.

Marvin Minsky

Helped shape early AI research and co-founded the MIT AI Laboratory.
